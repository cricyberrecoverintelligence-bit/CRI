About Cyber Recovery Intelligence

CRI (Cyber Recovery Intelligence) is a mission-driven NGO specializing in advanced criminal investigation, forensic analysis, and intelligence operations. We are building trusted partnerships with law enforcement agencies and communities through reliable, impact-focused support.
