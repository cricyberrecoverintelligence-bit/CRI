Cyber Fraud Recovery

CRI specializes in resolving cyber fraud incidents and recovering compromised financial assets. Our experts rapidly detect breaches, restore systems, and coordinate with investigators for fraud remediation.

We trace fraud paths, secure evidence for legal action, and implement controls to reduce future exposure.

Forensic Investigation

Our forensic team performs deep digital analysis on devices, systems, and networks to reconstruct crime timelines. We collect and preserve evidence with full chain-of-custody procedures for court and internal audits.

Services include disk forensics, log analysis, malware dissection, and advanced threat attribution.

Evaluation

CRI conducts comprehensive security and investigative evaluations to assess organizational vulnerabilities and readiness. Our specialists review policies, procedures, and systems to identify gaps in crime prevention and fraud detection capabilities.

We deliver actionable recommendations, risk assessments, and strategic guidance to strengthen your agency's investigative infrastructure and protect against emerging threats.
